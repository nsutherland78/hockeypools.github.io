Rules

Instructions:

At the beginning of the playoffs, participants must select the teams who they believe will win each of the best of 7 matchups in the Quarterfinals, and the number of games each team will win the matchup in (4, 5, 6 or 7 games).  This will only be done on a per round basis at the beginning of that round (not for the whole bracket).  Points for the correct number of games will only be awarded if the team they have chosen wins that matchup. Participants will also select the team that they believe will win the Stanley Cup (Final Round) at the beginning.  The chosen Stanley Cup Winner cannot be modified once the playoffs begin, and the Stanley Cup team chosen by each participant to win the playoffs must continue to be chosen by that participant in every matchup they appear, until they are eliminated.

Once the Quarterfinal round is complete, points will be tallied as per the below and carried forward to the next round. Once the new matchups are determined, participants will then select the teams who they believe will win each of the best of 7 matchups in the Semifinal round, and the number of games they will win the matchup in (4, 5, 6 or 7 games).

The same format will be followed for the Conference finals, and the Final.

Once the playoffs (Final round) are complete and the Stanley Cup winner is known, the sum of points for each participant from each round, and points for the correct Stanley Cup team will be tallied.  Participants will be awarded as per the rewards below.

*** If you do not submit your picks for each round before the puck drops in the first game of each round, you will be disqualified with no reimbursement ***

For more information on current format of the NHL playoffs, please reference the following link:

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stanley_Cup_playoffs#Current_format

Point Tally:

Quarterfinals:

2 points for correct team
2 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

16 points for choosing only correct teams.
32 points total available in the round.

Semifinals:

3 points for correct team
3 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

12 points for choosing only correct teams.
24 points total available in the round.

Conference Finals:

4 points for correct team
4 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

8 points for choosing only correct teams.
16 points total available in the round.

Final:

5 points for correct team
5 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

5 points for choosing only correct team.
10 points total available in the round.

Stanley Cup winner:
 

You will be given 5 points per round for each round that the participants Stanley Cup Winner progresses.  The points will be awarded at the end of that round.  This will total 20 points, with the last 5 points being awarded to participants who chose the correct Stanley Cup Winner. You can choose against your Stanley Cup winner, however you forfeit the Stanley Cup winner points awarded for that team that round, and all future rounds.

Tie Breaker:

1st Tie Breaker: Correct Stanley Cup Winner
2nd Tie Breaker: Most Correct Matchups Chosen (Team and # of Games)
3rd Tie Breaker: Most Correct Matchups (Team and # of Games) in the Last Round of the Playoffs

If none of the above tie breakers are capable of determining a single 1st, 2nd or 3rd, then the winnings for each placement will be split evenly between the tied competitors for said place.
